About Us

Resilience from Within offers a comprehensive and sustainable approach to addressing the economic and mental health challenges faced by women and youth in Northern Uganda. Through a combination of mindfulness practices, business development training, value-added agricultural skills and provision of access to agricultural innovation and input, the program aims to build inner strength and foster outer change within the community. By empowering participants with the tools to develop micro-enterprises, equipping them to process and market their agricultural products, and providing mental health support, this initiative addresses the root causes of poverty, unemployment, and trauma.
