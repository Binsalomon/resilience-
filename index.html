Harnessing mindfulness to spark entrepreneurial growth and transform lives

Through mindfulness, we unlock potential and guide individuals to build thriving enterprises, fostering resilience and lasting change in their communities.

Welcome to Resilience From Within (RFW) Resilience from Within (RFW) a community-driven initiative dedicated to building the inner capacities of women and men in Northern Uganda. We empower individuals with the tools to foster resilience, develop leadership, and embrace innovative solutions that break the cycle of poverty. Through a unique blend of mindfulness training and entrepreneurship development, RFW self-reliance and creates thriving, sustainable communities.
Resilience from Within (RFW) is dedicated to building inner capacities in women and men, fostering resilience to end poverty in Northern Uganda. Through mindfulness, entrepreneurship training, and sustainable development, we empower individuals to transform their lives and communities.

How We Build Resilience to End Poverty


Agricultural Innovation: Modern, sustainable farming techniques will be introduced alongside the provision of essential agricultural inputs, such as high-quality seeds, organic fertilizers, and tools. These resources will help boost productivity, diversify crops, and secure food availability, while creating surpluses for market sales. This holistic approach aims to improve both environmental sustainability and economic resilience within farming communities.

Micro-Enterprise Development: Provide comprehensive training in business skills, financial literacy, and market strategies, enabling participants to create value-added agricultural products and non-farm businesses.

Read More →

Savings and Investment: Promote a culture of saving, where participants are guided to save for their own equipment and to reinvest their earnings into their businesses for long-term sustainability.

Read More →

Mindfulness Practices: Address the psychological trauma resulting from years of conflict through ongoing mindfulness, mental health support and community healing initiatives.

Read More →

Youth Empowerment: Equip young people with vocational and entrepreneurial skills, preparing them to become the next generation of business leaders and skilled workers.

Read More →

Women’s Economic Empowerment: Prioritize the inclusion of women, encouraging their leadership and acknowledging their pivotal role in community development.

Read More →


Resilience from Within will adopt a holistic, community-driven approach to post-conflict recovery. Through investing in the skills, mental health, and economic well-being of women and youth in Northern Uganda, we aim to catalyze transformation from dependency to self-reliance, trauma to resilience, and poverty to prosperity. This program seeks to foster leadership from within the community while laying a foundation for sustainable change for future generations.

Our Approach

At the heart of our mission is empowering communities through sustainable programs. We focus on improving livelihoods, fostering entrepreneurship, and enabling leadership for lasting impact.

Our Impact in Numbers

  • 200+ farmers supported through seed programs.
  • 10 farming groups trained in mindfulness and leadership.
  • 50 micro-enterprises started by women and youth.
  • 75% improved income levels for participants.

These metrics are projections based on the initial phase of our program.
